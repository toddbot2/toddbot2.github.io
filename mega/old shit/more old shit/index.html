The year is 2023. After the failure of the short lived Trump administration, and the rise of the Elizabeth Warren-Rachel Maddow administration, America’s state governments have been disbanded and replaced by the 7 mega regions of North America. Each of these mega regions are unique economic power centers that are no longer dependent on state lines. Seven cities within each region stand as the major city hub politically and financially. An iconic titan of each region’s industry serves as the face of the region. The icon’s different backgrounds unify the regions and contribute to the national feeling of shared identity.
