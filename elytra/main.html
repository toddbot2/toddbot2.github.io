Our environment nourshes and protects us in life.

How should we respect that in death?

Everyday, more than 6500 people die in the United States. There is an ever-growing need for land allocated to those who have passed on. With the growing popularity of green-burials, that need is being passed on to the forests. The trees can not keep up with the need. Burning the deceased at the rate of those who pass has exposed the living to harmful toxins and darkend our skies. There is a need for a new solution.

Elytra

COMPLETE THE CYCLE

Elytra is a responsible body reintegration service that serves as an environmentally-friendly alternative to cremation and burial. Our bodies are composed of elements and compounds that came from the earth, animals, and plants we all lived in and around our entire lives.

REINTEGRATION PROCESS

The body is received, and gently washed

Spores remove toxins from the body

Beetles begin decompsition

Synthetic material is recycled

Bones are reduced to powder

Relics are returned
