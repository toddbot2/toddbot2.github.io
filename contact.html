Who Am I?

I can break shit apart and learn how it works. Systematic, procedural, and creative. I’m like the X-Men’s Cyclops, if he wasn’t stiff, lame, and one dimensional... Also not the leader of a group yet... You know what? I’m more like Donatello from the Ninja Turtles. Loves machines, pizza, high-fiving, fighting for what is right, and is all about his community.

What do I do?

I was a scentist in another life. I bring what I learned in that field to everything I do. As I added visual language to my vocabulary, I found that many of the concepts and processes from the world of science carried over to visual, product, and experience design.

Figuring out how things work keeps me up at night. The transition between the overarching concepts that lead to the nitty-gritty details is where my biggest assets live. The thrill I get when a bunch of processes turns into a product that convinces me keeps me glued to what I’m doing.
