Terence McKenna Longform Web Article

We were tasked with creating a longform web-based article. My role was to create the website that everything would live within. I wanted to make something that was simple enough to let the story and visuals show through, but had enough visual hook to keep the reader engaged. I built a HTML framework to flow copy in, and made spaces for images and page breaks. I modified some JQuery plugins that added some elements of motion and structure to the page.
